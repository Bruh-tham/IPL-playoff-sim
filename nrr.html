NRR Calculator Guide

Type the newest match stats into the input fields and click Add. It will automatically update the Live NRR and push the changes to the Points Table.

  • RS Runs Scored
  • OF Overs Faced
  • RC Runs Conceded
  • OB Overs Bowled

Over Math: Standard cricket overs convert automatically (e.g., typing 19.4 seamlessly converts to 19.666 for math).

All Out Rule: If a team is bowled out, their overs faced legally count as exactly 20.0 regardless of actual balls faced. Ensure you enter 20 if an all-out occurs.

Points Table Guide

Use the + and - arrow buttons to adjust the standings on the fly. The row will dynamically sort and flash yellow so you can easily track where your edited team jumped to.

  • MP Matches Played (Max 14)
  • W Wins (Awards 2 pts)
  • L Losses (Awards 0 pts)
  • NR No Result (Awards 1 pt)
  • Pts Total Points (Auto-Calculated)

The LIVE NRR column cannot be edited here. It is strictly mirrored from the Live NRR Calculator table on the left side of the dashboard to ensure mathematical accuracy.
